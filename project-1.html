COLLEGE COLAB

Developed an online platform that provides researchers with relevant details about research grants and allows organizations to post, edit, and delete their projects.

View Demo

Project Demo




Project Overview

• Online platform using ReactJS, MongoDB, NodeJS, and ExpressJS for researchers to post, edit, and delete research papers/projects and seek collaboration.

• User-friendly interface with features like applying, liking, and commenting on posts. User profile page displaying performance metrics to encourage productivity and recognition.
